Our Services

Here's what we can offer you

At AquaFix we base our prices through our 3 tiered system. The smaller the job, the lower the price. The bigger the job, the higher the price. Check out our three categories below to get an idea of what you could be paying!

In-n-out

What is an In-n-out job?

In-n-out jobs are small jobs that generally require just one visit and are easy and simple to resolve.

Fix small leak

Blocked drain

Blocked sink

£30

* Can vary with severity and location *

Complex

What is a Complex job?

Complex jobs are more involved jobs that may require one or more call backs. These are jobs that are more time consuming and slightly harder than in-n-out jobs and mainly consist of installations.

Installing shower pump

Installing taps

Installing toilets

Installing dishwasher

Replacing leaking copper pipework

£70

* Can vary with severity and location *

Emergency

What is an Emergency job?

An emergency job consist of dire situations that must be fixed immediately. This is why we have plumbers ready to go 24/7.

Flood clean-up

Burst pipework

No water supply

£200

* Can vary with severity and location *
